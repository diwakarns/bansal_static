About us

Bansal Eco Energy is a prominent manufacturer of Biomass Pellets headquartered in Bikaner, Rajasthan.

Biomass Pellets are deemed to be environmentally friendly and we are fully committed to our patrons and the society to provide only optimum quality products.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility located in Rajasthan is powered by solar energy to reduce our carbon footprint and is handled effectively by a team of experts.

What are Biomass
Wood Pellets?

Biomass is organic matter that can be used to generate energy. It can include wood, plant materials and even animal dung, used to fuel fires for warmth and cooking.
